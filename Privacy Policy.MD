## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Script Hub Bot ("Bot", "Service", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with the Bot. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data

- **User Identifiers:** Your Roblox user ID or username, used to identify you within the Service
- **Command Usage Data:** Records of commands you execute, including timestamps and parameters
- **Platform Metadata:** Device type, operating system, or client version where applicable

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Payment or financial information
- Real names, email addresses, or physical addresses
- Private messages or chat logs outside of Bot interactions
- Browsing history or activity outside the Service

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

- **Service Operation:** To process your commands and deliver scripts
- **Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve the Bot's features and performance
- **Moderation:** To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse
- **Debugging:** To identify and resolve technical issues

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

- **Legal Obligation:** If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
- **Safety:** To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public
- **Service Providers:** If we use third-party hosting or infrastructure providers, they may process data on our behalf solely to operate the Service

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secured servers with reasonable technical safeguards
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized operators only
- We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
- While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security

## 6. Data Retention

- Usage logs and command data may be retained for up to 90 days for operational purposes
- User identifiers may be retained for the duration of your use of the Service
- Upon request, we will make reasonable efforts to delete your data (see Section 9)

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party services, and we encourage you to review their policies independently.
